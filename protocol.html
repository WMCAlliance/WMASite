We feel that consistent ban lengths and rules are important.

  • A ban is decided upon by a staff member only - players are not to "recommend" a ban
  • Bans are on a 3 strike policy - meaning your 3rd ban is permanent
  • The length of a ban is determined by the amount of damage caused - larger grief means a longer ban
  • The standard length of a temporary ban is 7 days
  • At least 1 image of the grief caused must be provided as proof at the time of banning - this can include screenshots of chat or private messages
